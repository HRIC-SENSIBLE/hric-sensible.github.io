WP1: Project Management

Overall project coordination, administrative and financial management, quality assurance, and reporting to Innovation Fund Denmark.

Duration: M1-M42 | Lead: SDU

WP2: Energy Harvesting System

Design and development of a lightweight energy harvester capable of tapping energy from high-voltage powerlines to charge drone batteries without infrastructure modifications.

Duration: M1-M36 | Lead: Fraunhofer

WP3: AI-Based Inspection Algorithms

Development of deep learning algorithms for automated powerline inspection, defect detection, and data optimization to store only relevant inspection data onboard.

Duration: M1-M36 | Lead: SDU

WP4: Drone Swarm Communication

Implementation of low-power LoRaWAN-based communication network enabling collaborative drone operations along powerline corridors.

Duration: M6-M30 | Lead: Aarhus University

WP5: System Integration

Integration of energy harvesting, AI algorithms, and swarm communication into a unified autonomous inspection drone platform.

Duration: M24-M42 | Lead: SDU

WP6: Field Testing & Validation

Real-world testing of the integrated system on actual powerline infrastructure in collaboration with utility partners.

Duration: M30-M42 | Lead: Geopartner

WP7: Commercialization

Development of business models, market analysis, and commercial roadmap for bringing Drones4Energy technologies to market.

Duration: M12-M42 | Lead: Science Ventures Denmark
