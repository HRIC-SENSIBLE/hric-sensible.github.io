Event – 31st of May 2022

Invitation to the Final Drones for Energy Inspection workshop, At the HCAA Airport in Odense, Denmark.

Please save the date for the final workshop in the Drones4Energy project.

The workshop will present the achievements of the IFD Drones4Energy project (https://drones4energy.dk/) in drone development for grasping high-voltage cables, AI for powerline inspection, and energy harvesting for drones recharging, and a swarm system for fast inspection. The workshop involves talks from the project partners and will be running in a hybrid setup with live demos. For registration and more details contact: akl@tek.sdu.dk.

Event – 15th of November 2021

Drones4Energy Mid-Project Review Meeting

The project consortium gathered for the mid-project review with Innovation Fund Denmark. The meeting showcased progress on energy harvesting prototypes, AI algorithms, and drone swarm coordination systems.

Event – 20th of June 2020

First Successful Energy Harvesting Test

Fraunhofer IMS and SDU successfully demonstrated the first prototype of the lightweight energy harvester capable of extracting power from high-voltage lines. The test was conducted at a controlled facility in Germany.

Event – 10th of December 2019

Annual Project Workshop at SDU

All project partners convened at the University of Southern Denmark for the first annual workshop. Topics included progress reports from all work packages and planning for the upcoming year.

Event – 1st of December 2018

Project Kick-off Meeting

The Drones4Energy project officially commenced with a kick-off meeting at SDU UAS Centre. Representatives from all partner organizations attended to establish project governance and initial work plans.
