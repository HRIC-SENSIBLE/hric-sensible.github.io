News and Events

Workshop – October 2025

IROS 2025 Workshop at Hangzhou, China

Second Human Modeling Workshop: AI-Powered Human Modeling for Healthcare Robotics

The SENSIBLE project team organized a workshop at the IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS) 2025 in Hangzhou, China. The workshop focused on AI-powered human modeling techniques and their applications in healthcare robotics, building on the research developments from the SENSIBLE project.

Workshop – May 2024

ICRA 2024 Workshop at Yokohama, Japan

First Human Modeling Workshop: Innovations and Applications of Human Modeling in Physical Human-Robot Interaction

The project team organized the first Human Modeling Workshop at the IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA) 2024 in Yokohama, Japan. This workshop brought together researchers from around the world to discuss the latest advances in human modeling for physical human-robot interaction, a core research topic of the SENSIBLE project.

Milestone – 2024

Integration 1 & 2 Completed

The SENSIBLE project successfully completed the first and second system integrations (D7.1 and D7.2), demonstrating the teleoperation system capabilities for wind turbine blade maintenance tasks.

Event – March 1, 2023

Project Kick-off Meeting

The SENSIBLE project officially commenced with a kick-off meeting. All partners – University of Southern Denmark, Aarhus University, Clobotics, and Innopixel – gathered to establish project governance, define initial work plans, and set the foundation for the 48-month research collaboration funded by Innovation Fund Denmark.
