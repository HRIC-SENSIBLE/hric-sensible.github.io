Project at a glance

SENSIBLE is a research project focused on developing symbiotic teleoperation technologies for safe and cost-efficient wind turbine blade maintenance. The project aims to revolutionize the way wind turbine maintenance is conducted through advanced robotics and human-robot collaboration.

Key Features

Teleoperation

Advanced teleoperation systems enabling remote control of maintenance robots for wind turbine blade inspection and repair.

AI-Assisted Maintenance

Machine learning algorithms to assist operators in detecting defects and optimizing maintenance procedures.

Safety Systems

Comprehensive safety systems ensuring secure operations during wind turbine blade maintenance at height.

Cost Efficiency

Reducing maintenance costs through innovative robotic solutions and optimized operational procedures.
